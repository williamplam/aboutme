Exit 5A Robotics

Exit 5A Robotics was started in 2014 and currently is active today, collaborating with Exit 5 Robotics.

Find out more at:

  • https://www.instagram.com/exit5aroboticsofficial/
  • https://www.livingstonrobotics.org/teams/fll/exit-5a-robotics/
  • https://www.facebook.com/exit5arobotics/

Awards:

  • 2020/21 VEX AI Competition (VAIC) Pilot Program
  • 2019 WRO (World Robot Olympiad) US National Championships - 2nd Place Regular Senior Category
  • 2018/2019 FLL World Festival - 3rd Place Inspiration Award
  • 2018/2019 FLL World Festival - Coach/Mentor of the Year Award
  • 2018/2019 FLL NJ State Championships - 1st Place Champions Award
  • 2018 WRO (World Robot Olympiad) US National Championships - 1st Place Design Award Senior Category
    • 3rd Place Regular Senior Category
  • 2017/2018 LegoLand FLL North American Open Championship - Judges Award
    • 14th place Robot Performance
