Women In Engineering Day 2018, 2019, 2022

Bill Lam

To capture the growing movement of all-girls robotics teams in 2017, we commenced an effort to bring these teams from spanning all competition venues and all age groups into one place so that they could showcase their efforts. Research has shown that the best way to encourage young women to enter STEM fields to for contact and exposure with older role models in STEM fields that identify as female.
